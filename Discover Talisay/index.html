Discover Talisay, Batangas

Seedling Bowl of the Nation

Talisay, Batangas, is dubbed the “Seedling Bowl of the Nation” as it produces 80 to 85% of seedlings and saplings in the Philippines. This remarkable achievement highlights the town’s rich and fertile lands, perfect for cultivating a diverse range of flora. To celebrate this amazing feat, the Talisay LGU drummed up the PunLAD Festival, a portmanteau of the phrase punla sa pag-unlad (seed for prosperity). The festival showcases the ingenuity and hard work of local farmers, bringing the community together to honor their agricultural heritage.

During the festival, visitors can participate in various activities, including seed planting workshops and local cuisine tastings. This vibrant celebration not only promotes sustainable farming practices but also fosters a sense of pride in Talisay’s agricultural identity. Locals of Talisay take pride in their products and continue to innovate their methods to serve the Filipinos and contribute to the town’s development and prosperity. The lush landscapes and favorable climate make it an ideal location for agricultural activities.

Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a history buff, or just looking for a relaxing escape, Talisay has something for everyone. Explore our vibrant local culture, indulge in traditional cuisine, and enjoy the warm hospitality that our community is known for. Experience the beauty and charm of Talisay, where every seed planted is a step towards a greener future.
