Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish is an American singer-songwriter who rose to fame at a young age, known for her ethereal voice and genre-defying sound. Born on December 18, 2001, in Los Angeles, California, Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell was exposed to music early, growing up in a family of musicians and actors. At the age of 13, Billie uploaded her first single, "Ocean Eyes," to SoundCloud, which went viral and marked the beginning of her career. Her debut album, "When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?" released in 2019, was a commercial and critical success. The album's unique mix of dark pop, alternative, and electropop resonated with audiences and set her apart from other artists in the industry.
